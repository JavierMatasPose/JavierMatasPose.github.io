**Master's Thesis & Entrepreneurship**

I developed and implemented an artificial intelligence in a financial market simulation environment, capable of automatically buying and selling shares through a real broker.

*   **Technology:** Based on **Deep Reinforcement Learning** (DRL), taking inspiration from AlphaZero and AlphaTensor.
*   **Results:** Traded automatically on the Nasdaq, achieving returns of up to **47.3%**.
*   **Recognition:** 
    *   **Top 3 Award** at the international Robotrader algorithmic trading competition.
    *   Presented at the **Madrid Stock Exchange**.
